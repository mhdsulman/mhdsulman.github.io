    Textbook: Calculus Concepts & Contexts by James Stewart 4th Edition, 2010.

    Course Description: This course provides introduction to calculus of several variables. Topics covered include: vector functions and their derivatives, motion in two and three dimensions, lines, planes, and parametric surfaces, spherical and cylindrical coordinates. Partial derivatives and multiple integrals in two and three dimensions. Vector fields, line and surface integrals. Green's, Divergence, and Stokes' theorems.
