    Course Description: This is the second part of a two-semester series of numerical analysis courses. It provides an introduction to the analysis and numerical methods for solving partial differential equations. Topics covered include multistep methods for initial value problems for ODEs, introduction and classification of PDEs, finite difference solution of PDEs and finite element method.
