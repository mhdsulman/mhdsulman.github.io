    Lecture Notes
    Material for the lectures is available on Pilot

    Course Description: This course provides introduction to numerical linear algebra. Topics include: singular value decomposition; least squares problems and QR factorization; direct and iterative methods for solving linear systems; eigenvalues and eigenvectors computation.
