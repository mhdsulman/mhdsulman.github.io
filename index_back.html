Research & Teaching Interests

Computational and applied mathematics; numerical methods for linear and nonlinear systems, ordinary and partial differential equations, unconstrained optimization; applications to physical and statistical models

IQP Advising Interests

Environmental applications of mathematical modeling, applications of mathematical modeling to energy and resources, health care applications of mathematics and computing, mathematical models in resource management and planning, economic applications of mathematical modeling, computers & education

Recent Publications

  • Globalization techniques for Newton-Krylov methods and applications to the fully-coupled solution of the Navier-Stokes equations, with R. P. Pawlowski, J. N. Shadid, and J. P. Simonis, SIAM Review, 48 (2006), 700-721.

  • On backtracking failure in Newton--GMRES methods, with R. S. Tuminaro and J. N. Shadid, J. Comp. Physics, 180 (2002), 549--558.

  • Solution methods for large-scale nonlinear problems: an evolving research area, with C.S. Woodward, SIAM News, 34 (April 2001), 15-16.

  • NITSOL: a Newton iterative solver for nonlinear systems, with M. Pernice, Special Issue on Iterative Methods, SIAM J. Sci. Comput., 19 (1998), 302--318.
