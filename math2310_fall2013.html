    Textbook:   Calculus Concepts & Contexts by James Stewart 4th Edition, 2010.
    Course Description:   This course provides an introduction to integration and its applications. Topics covered include: parametric curves, basic techniques of integration, volumes of revolution, arc length, sequences, series, 3D coordinate systems, vectors, equations of lines, planes and surfaces.
    Recommended background:    MTH 2300 -- Calculus I. Although the courses will make use of computers, no programming experience is assumed.
