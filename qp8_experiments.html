Time Dependent Quadrupole Model

  • Case I: $\mu_1 = \mu_2 = 0$ and $\alpha_x=\alpha_y = 0$



  • Case II: $\mu_1 = 1\quad \mu_2 = 1$ and $\alpha_x=\alpha_y = 0$



  • Case III: $\mu_1 = \mu_2 = 0$ and $\alpha_x=\alpha_y = \pi/4$



  • Case IV: $\mu_1 =1\quad \mu_2 = 1$ and $\alpha_x=\pi/4, \quad \alpha_y = \pi/4$



  • Case V: $\mu_1 = 1,\quad \mu_2 = 4$ and $\alpha_x=\pi/4, \quad \alpha_y = \pi/4$
