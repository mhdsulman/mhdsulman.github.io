    Textbook: Partial Differential Equations: An Introduction , 2nd edition, John Wiley & Sons, 2008.

    Course Description: This course provides an introduction to partial differential equations (PDEs) with some applications in physical and engineering sciences. Topics include: Linear first oder PDEs; Method of characteristics; Classification of second order PDEs; Separation of variables; Maximum principles; Green's functions; Finite difference methods (if time permits).
