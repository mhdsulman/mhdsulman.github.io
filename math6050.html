Course Description:  This is a graduate level course, and it provides mathematical techniques that are related to engineering disciplines. Topics covered include matrices, orthogonal functions and Fourier series, boundary value problems, numerical solutions of PDEs, and complex analysis.

 

Syllabus:

1. Matrices (~1.5 week).

2. Orthogonal functions and Fourier series (~1.5 weeks).

3. Boundary value problems (~4 weeks).

4. Numerical solutions of PDEs (~2 weeks).

5. Complex Analysis (4 weeks)
