Welcome

We welcome you to explore the College of Science and Mathematics' website. We offer a student-centered environment with lots of personal attention from faculty that take the time to work with you. Our students collaborate to solve real-world problems on a daily basis and can take the lessons learned in the classroom with them to their career employment. If you want to make a difference in society, check out our science programs and discover the wonderful world of science and mathematics.
